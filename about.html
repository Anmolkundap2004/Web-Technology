About BIET

Bapuji Institute of Engineering and Technology (BIET) is an autonomous institution established in 1979, located in Davangere, Karnataka. The institution aims to provide excellent education and produce competent engineers who are prepared to meet the challenges of the rapidly evolving world.

BIET is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. With a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and holistic development, the institute strives to shape the future of technology and engineering.

Our Mission

To provide value-based quality education and develop students’ skills to make them successful engineers and leaders of tomorrow. The institution aims to foster an environment conducive to creativity, innovation, and research in the field of technology.

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and classrooms
  • Sports and recreation facilities
  • Sprawling campus with ample space
  • Well-equipped hostels and mess

Academic Excellence

BIET offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs, including courses in Computer Science, Electronics, Civil, Mechanical, and other disciplines. The institute is also home to various research centers and strives to foster an environment of academic excellence.
